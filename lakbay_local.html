ABOUT LAKBAY LOKAL

Lakbay Lokal is a celebration of Filipino entrepreneurship and cultural heritage through food and crafts. This event brings together local vendors, artisans, and food entrepreneurs to showcase their products in a festive marketplace setting. Visitors can sample authentic Filipino cuisine, purchase unique souvenirs, and experience the rich cultural diversity of the Philippines.

May 10, 2025

10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Located at the heart of the DLSU-D campus, Lakbay Lokal creates a vibrant marketplace atmosphere where visitors can explore Filipino culture through its diverse culinary traditions and craftsmanship.

  • Authentic Filipino cuisine from different regions
  • Handcrafted souvenirs and cultural items
  • Interactive cultural demonstrations
  • Support for local entrepreneurs and artisans

Our Partners

PARTNER CONCESSIONAIRES

Meet our amazing partner concessionaires who bring the best of Filipino food and crafts to Lakbay Lokal.

  • Sarap Pinoy

    Traditional Filipino Cuisine

    Sarap Pinoy offers authentic Filipino dishes from various regions, showcasing the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines. From adobo to kare-kare, experience the true taste of Filipino home cooking.

  • Kamay Crafts

    Handmade Filipino Souvenirs

    Kamay Crafts specializes in handcrafted souvenirs made by local artisans. Their products include woven items, wood carvings, and traditional Filipino accessories that showcase Filipino craftsmanship.

  • Kakanin Corner

    Traditional Filipino Desserts

    Kakanin Corner offers a variety of traditional Filipino rice cakes and desserts. From bibingka to puto and kutsinta, satisfy your sweet tooth with authentic Filipino delicacies.

  • Barako Brew

    Filipino Coffee & Beverages

    Barako Brew serves locally-sourced Filipino coffee and traditional beverages. Experience the rich taste of Kapeng Barako and other refreshing Filipino drinks like sago't gulaman and buko juice.

Recognition

SPECIAL AWARDS

Lakbay Lokal celebrates excellence in Filipino entrepreneurship and cultural representation through these special awards.

  • Best Food Concessionaire: ₱5,000 + Trophy + Feature in DLSU-D Newsletter
  • Best Souvenir Concessionaire: ₱5,000 + Trophy + Feature in DLSU-D Newsletter
  • Most Innovative Product: ₱3,000 + Certificate + Marketing Support
  • Best Cultural Representation: ₱3,000 + Certificate + Marketing Support
  • People's Choice Award: ₱3,000 + Certificate + Priority Booth at Next Event
  • Most Sustainable Practices: ₱3,000 + Certificate + Feature on Social Media

All concessionaires are automatically entered into consideration for these awards. Winners will be announced at the closing ceremony of the event.
