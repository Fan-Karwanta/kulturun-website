KultuRUN

KultuRUN is an Eco-Tourism themed Fun Run where participants would be able to explore the university campus, and experience the rich Filipino culture through vibrant themed water stations inspired by many colorful and culturally significant festivals throughout the Philippines.

Pawshionista, the Sustainable Pet Fashion Show at DLSU-D Grandstand will provide a fun and engaging platform where pet owners can creatively dress-up their pets in Filipino culture / festival-inspired outfits made from upcycled / sustainable materials.

12:30nn - 3:30 PM

3rd Event

LAKBAY LOKAL

The Lakbay Lokal / Local Business Bazaar at DLSU-D Track Oval will showcase an array selection of locally made products and cuisine, promoting Filipino craftsmanship and sustainability. The vibrant bazaars will showcase the creativity and artistry of Filipino entrepreneurs through providing a wide range of environmentally responsible products, and traditional delicacies—featuring products that reflect eco-conscious practices.

May 10, 2025

11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

  • Local Artisan Products
  • Traditional Filipino Cuisine
  • Eco-friendly Merchandise
  • Cultural Demonstrations

Event Guidelines

Mechanics & Instructions

May 10, 2025

KultuRUN Mechanics

  • Participants must wear their official racing bib in front and visible at all times to avoid an unrecorded finish.
  • The official event singlet must be worn throughout the run.
  • Races include 1 km, 3 km, and 5 km categories, with different start times (5K at 4:00 pm, 3K at 4:30 pm, 1K at 5:00 pm).
  • Participants with their pets joining the 1km (pet run) must be responsible and keep their pets with them at all times.
  • Finishers are identified by gun time, from the gun start until crossing the finish line.
Register Now
May 10, 2025

Pawshionista Mechanics

  • Registration is from 12:00 NN to 1:00 PM; participants must be ready before the competition begins.
  • Each participant will only be given a 15 - minute grace period from the start of the program to be present. otherwise, they will be disqualified.
  • Each pet owner can register only one pet (cat or dog).
  • Pets must wear Filipino-inspired outfits made from recycled materials to promote sustainability.
  • Each participant together with their pets will be given a maximum of 3 minutes on stage to showcase and share details about their sustainable wear.

Lakbay Lokal Layout

Lakbay Lokal Mechanics

  • Each booth has a designated space; exceeding this space will result in point deductions.
  • Concessionaires will be evaluated on a single criterion, with clear voting instructions provided.
  • Voting will take place throughout the event, with 2nd-year BTM students and attendees acting as judges.
  • Each participant receives two stickers to vote for their preferred booths.
