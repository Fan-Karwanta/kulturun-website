PAWSHIONISTA

Pawshionista, the Sustainable Pet Fashion Show at DLSU-D Grandstand will provide a fun and engaging platform where pet owners can creatively dress-up their pets in Filipino culture / festival-inspired outfits made from upcycled / sustainable materials.

Event Details

ABOUT PAWSHIONISTA

Pawshionista is a sustainable pet fashion show that celebrates Filipino culture through creative pet fashion. Pet owners will showcase their beloved companions dressed in festival-inspired outfits made from upcycled materials, promoting both cultural appreciation and environmental sustainability.

May 10, 2025

12:30nn - 3:30 PM

Categories

COMPETITION CATEGORIES

  • Small Dogs

    Up to 10kg

    For small breed dogs weighing up to 10kg. Perfect for showcasing intricate designs on our smaller furry friends.

  • Medium/Large Dogs

    Over 10kg

    For medium to large breed dogs weighing over 10kg. Showcase grand designs on our bigger companions.

  • Cats

    All Cats

    For all our feline friends. Showcase your cat's personality through creative sustainable fashion.

Event Guidelines

COMPETITION GUIDELINES

General Rules

  • Maximum of 3 minutes stage time per participant
  • Outfit must be made from sustainable recycled/upcycled materials
  • 1 Owner / 1 Pet ONLY per entry
  • Matching wear for the owner and pet is optional but encouraged
  • Pets must be on leash/harness at all times except during stage performance
  • Pet owners are responsible for their pet's behavior and waste management

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity and Originality (30%)
  • Use of Sustainable Materials (25%)
  • Filipino Cultural Elements (20%)
  • Overall Presentation and Stage Presence (15%)
  • Audience Impact (10%)
