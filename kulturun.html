ABOUT KULTURUN

KultuRUN is an eco-tourism themed fun run that celebrates Filipino culture through a unique running experience. Participants will explore the beautiful DLSU-D campus while encountering vibrant themed water stations inspired by various Filipino festivals. This event combines fitness, cultural appreciation, and environmental awareness in a colorful and engaging way.

May 10, 2025

5:00 AM - Assembly Time

Join us for a morning of fun, fitness, and Filipino culture as we run through themed stations representing the Philippines' most colorful festivals. Each station offers a unique cultural experience and refreshment for runners.

Event Guidelines


The KultuRUN will be open to the general public, from students, to educators, Family and Pet Communities, Run enthusiasts and fanatics. Small and Medium-sized businesses and enterprises will be invited to take part in the bazaar, providing them with an opportunity to showcase and earn profit from their products.

KultuRUN is scheduled on Saturday, May 10, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 7:00 PM at DLSU-D Track Oval and throughout the campus.

All participants are required to complete the registration online via Google Forms posted on the official Facebook page.

Registration Process

  • All participants must complete the registration process via Google Forms, which will be posted on the official Facebook page of KultuRUN.
  • Participants must fill out the registration form, including personal and safety-related information.
  • For Lasallian Students and Faculty: Name, Age, Birthday, Contact Information, CYS (Course, Year, Section), Student ID, Outlook Email, College Department, GCash Number, Gcash Name, GCash Receipt, Gcash Reference Number, Emergency Contact Person, Emergency Contact Number
  • Others: Name, Age, Birthday, Contact Information, One (1) Valid ID/Student ID, GCash Number, Gcash Name, GCash Receipt, Gcash Reference Number, Emergency Contact Person, Emergency Contact Number
  • To confirm registration, participants must pay the required fee via GCash.
  • Upon successful payment, participants must upload proof of payment (e.g., screenshot of receipt) in the Google Form.
  • Upon successful registration, participants will receive a confirmation email with race details and kit collection instructions.
  • Race kits include a bib number, singlet, and other necessary items.
  • Participants must collect their kits at the assigned pickup location before the event.
  • To validate registration, participants must present their confirmation receipt and a valid ID upon kit collection.
